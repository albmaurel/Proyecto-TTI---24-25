tuple< double, double, double > Geodetic ( Matrix r)

Converts Cartesian coordinates to geodetic coordinates (longitude, latitude, altitude).

This function transforms a position vector in Cartesian coordinates (ECEF) to geodetic coordinates (longitude, latitude, and altitude) based on the WGS-84 ellipsoid parameters. The conversion uses an iterative method to account for the Earth's flattening.

Parameters
rA 3-element Matrix representing the Cartesian coordinates (X, Y, Z) in meters.
Returns
A tuple containing:
  • longitude (radians)
  • latitude (radians)
  • altitude above the ellipsoid (meters)
